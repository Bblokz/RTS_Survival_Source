
setting: east germany.
hills; factory with many unmanned nearly vehicles parked around;

the player must make sure the factory survives the soviet assault.

all marders or stugs (depending on what the player picked) are available for capture from the start of the mission.

later large formations of t-34s and kvs will appear.
